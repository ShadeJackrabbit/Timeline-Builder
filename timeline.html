Wheat Cultivation Begins

-8000

In northern Mesopotamia, now northern Iraq, cultivation of barley and wheat begins. At first they are used for beer, gruel, and soup, eventually for bread.

Neolithic Subpluvial in North Africa

-7900

The Sahara desert region supports a savanna-like environment. Lake Chad is larger than the current Caspian Sea.

8.2 Kiloyear Event

-6200

A sudden decrease of global temperatures, likely caused by the final collapse of the Laurentide ice sheet which leads to drier conditions in East Africa and Mesopotamia.
